History/Colonial Life Passport Assignment 1
Writing to England
You are a child in 1646 visiting with your cousins, Elizabeth and Samuel.
Write a postcard to your relatives back in England describing your day at the Saugus Iron Works.
You have learned a lot about how the Iron Works operated.
Create your own picture postcard (5x7") or you may go to Saugus.net for a choice of picture postcards of the Saugus Iron Works.
About how many miles do you think your postcard will travel to England?
