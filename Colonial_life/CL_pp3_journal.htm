Colonial Life Passport Assignment 3
You have just spent the day with Elizabeth and Samuel in 1646. Use the My Daily Journal page (see teacher) and write a realistic account of your day. Write in first person and use descriptive language.

The Time Warp Machine has a malfunction. You are stuck in 1646. Write another journal page extending your visit with Samuel and Elizabeth. Write in first person. Include information about your clothing, meals, games, work, and schooling. You may illustrate your journal entry.
You are applying for a job at the Iron Works. Write a letter of application. Include details about your ironmaking training and previous work experience. What makes you the best candidate for the job? Name your desired weekly salary.
