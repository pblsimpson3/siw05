Rocks and Minerals Passport Assignment 3
Write an Autobiography for a Rock Person.
Your rock essay will be a page of a family album entitled It's a Hard Rock Life.

The Autobiography of Molly Metamorphic Rock
The Autobiography of Sam Sedimentary Rock
examples

• Your essay must be written in the first person and should contain at least two to three paragraphs.
• The introductory paragraph should explain how your rock person was formed. Be scientifically correct.
• Use information about the rock cycle and the three types of rock.
• In the following paragraphs be creative. Tell about your early years, likes, dislikes, hobbies, and strengths.
BONUS: For extra credit have your rock person come from the Saugus Iron Works.
Now that your autobiography is completed, it is time to create a three dimensional (3-D) rock person. Select a rock that has some of the characteristics of your rock person. Add features such as eyes, nose, mouth, hair and clothing that fit the characteristics. HAVE FUN!
